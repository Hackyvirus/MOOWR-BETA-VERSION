Capital Goods

Please provide the information of import of the goods in individual lines considering the different customs duty rate structures (BCD, AIDC, ADD, IGST). To clarify :

  • If out of three machines, two have the applicable BCD @10% and IGST @18%, but third machine has BCD @10% but IGST @28%, the values of two machines having same rate structure to be clubbed and entered as a single entry, whereas the values of third machine to be entered as a separate line item.
  • Similarly, if two machines are being imported,one having BCD @5% and second having BCD @10%, with both having IGST @18%,due to varying rate structure, details of each machine to be entered as a separate line item

The default period of the project life cycle is presumed at 10 years.

The annual growth of raw material imports & sales is presumed at 5% per year. The calculation of net benefits is worked out accordingly.
